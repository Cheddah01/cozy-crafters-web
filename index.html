✦ What We're About

Not a mega-server.
Just a good one.

Cozy Crafters is a small survival multiplayer server built around one idea: play with people you actually like being around. No pay-to-win, no 200-player lobbies, no 14-year-old moderators on a power trip. Just a handful of people building stuff, losing stuff, and blaming each other for the stuff that went missing.

⛏️

Pure Survival

Vanilla-adjacent gameplay on Paper 1.21. Quality-of-life plugins, no gameplay-breaking nonsense. You mine it, you keep it.

🏘️

Tight Community

Small enough that everyone knows each other. Big enough that someone's always online doing something questionable near your base.

📰

The Cozy Chronicles

Our very own in-game newspaper covering server drama, build showcases, and the weekly crimes of one player known only as Cheddah.

✦ How To Join

Three steps. That's it.

We keep it simple because we're not running an airport. Join the Discord, fill out a quick app so we know you're not going to burn everything down (on purpose), and hop on.

1

Join Our Discord

All server communication, announcements, and casual chaos lives here. It's also where you'll apply.

2

Submit an App

Quick and painless. Tell us a bit about yourself and what you like to do in Minecraft. We're not picky, just cautious.

3

Get Whitelisted

Once you're approved, you'll get the server IP and can jump right in. Welcome home.
